Technology designed to give back.

AngelSpoon is a nonprofit initiative providing advanced stabilization at no cost to those who receive it. It supports smoother, more controlled eating for individuals with Parkinson’s disease. The sleek, responsive design helps reduce the impact of involuntary movement, making everyday meals easier and more comfortable.

How We Stand Out

We use advanced motion-stabilization technology and thoughtful design to help reduce the effects of hand tremors and support steadier eating.

AngelSpoon

AngelSpoon provides thoughtfully designed stabilization support to help individuals with Parkinson’s disease eat more comfortably and confidently. Distributed free of cost through our nonprofit program, the device focuses on accessibility, ease of use, and improving quality of life at every meal.

AngelSpoon 2.0 (Under development)

AngelSpoon Smart is our next-generation, AI-powered stabilizing device currently in the R&D stage. Designed to adapt to movement patterns in real time, it aims to further reduce the impact of hand tremors through intelligent motion-stabilization technology, expanding our mission to deliver smarter, more responsive assistive solutions.
