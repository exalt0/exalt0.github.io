AngelSpoon

A stabilization utensil designed for steady, comfortable eating. Passive stabilization reduces unwanted motion without charging or calibration.

Support Distribution

Built for Stability

Weighted base shifts center of gravity downward so the utensil remains steady during natural hand motion.

Ergonomic Grip

Enlarged cylindrical handle supports a relaxed grip, reduces finger strain, and is easy to clean while remaining secure.

Interchangeable Heads

Detachable heads let you switch between spoon and fork quickly while preserving balance and stability.

Durable Construction

Constructed from food-safe, durable materials with a sealed internal weighting system to ensure longevity and cleanliness.

Designed for Everyday Meals

Engineered for everyday use — balanced, dependable, and easy to integrate into daily routines and dining settings.
