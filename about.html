Our Mission

At AngelSpoon, we are committed to helping people with Parkinson’s disease live with greater comfort, confidence, and independence by removing barriers to essential assistive support.

AngelSpoon is distributed through our nonprofit program to expand accessibility. We are committed to increasing availability and ensuring that practical support reaches those who can benefit from it.
